<p><p>The Marine Fuel Cell System market includes two main types: Polymer Electrolyte Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC) and Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC). PEMFCs operate at low temperatures, offering quick start-up and high efficiency, making them suitable for various marine applications. In contrast, SOFCs function at higher temperatures, providing greater efficiency for larger power outputs, ideal for ships with significant energy demands. Both fuel cell types contribute to cleaner marine energy solutions, reducing emissions and enhancing sustainability in maritime operations.</p></p>
