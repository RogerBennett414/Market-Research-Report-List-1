<p><p>The Raise Boring Rig market encompasses three main types: raise-boring, down-reaming, and box-holing. Raise-boring involves drilling vertical or inclined holes for ore passes and ventilation shafts, ensuring precision and stability. Down-reaming expands an existing borehole, enhancing its diameter efficiently and safely. Box-holing refers to the process of drilling vertical holes without requiring a casing, often used in underground access. Each method serves distinct applications in mining and tunneling, prioritizing safety, efficiency, and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
