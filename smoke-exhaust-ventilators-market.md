<p><p>The Smoke Exhaust Ventilators market is divided into two main types: Side Suction and Top Suction. Side Suction ventilators draw smoke horizontally from the sides, effectively controlling smoke spread in larger areas and ensuring improved safety in industrial settings. In contrast, Top Suction ventilators extract smoke vertically from the highest point, facilitating efficient smoke clearance in enclosed spaces like warehouses and tunnels. Both types are crucial for enhancing fire safety by improving air quality during emergencies.</p></p>
