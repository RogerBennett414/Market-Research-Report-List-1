<p><ul><li>Threshold Type</li><li>Linear Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hall-effect sensors are primarily categorized into two market types: threshold type and linear type. Threshold type sensors provide discrete output signals, activating when the magnetic field exceeds a specific level, commonly used for position detection and speed sensing. In contrast, linear type sensors produce an output that varies proportionally with the magnetic field strength, enabling precise measurements of current and position. Both types find applications across various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation, driven by their reliability and versatility.</p></p>
