<p><p>Micro blood flow biosensors encompass advanced technologies for monitoring blood circulation at a microscopic level. Piezoelectric sensor wires convert mechanical strain from blood flow into electrical signals, providing real-time data on hemodynamics. Pressure microcatheters, on the other hand, measure blood pressure and flow dynamics in small, delicate vessels, enabling precise diagnostics and interventions. Both types enhance the understanding of vascular health, aiding in early detection of cardiovascular conditions and improving patient outcomes through targeted therapy and monitoring.</p></p>
