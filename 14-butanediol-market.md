<p><strong>The 1,4-Butanediol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The 1,4-butanediol market includes several production processes. The Reppe process uses acetylene and formaldehyde, yielding high purity butanediol but at higher costs. The Davy process involves hydrogenation of maleic anhydride, while the Butadiene process derives from butadiene, benefiting from lower raw material costs. Lastly, the Propylene oxide process utilizes propylene oxide, offering a more sustainable and efficient route. Each method impacts market dynamics based on production efficiency, cost, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
