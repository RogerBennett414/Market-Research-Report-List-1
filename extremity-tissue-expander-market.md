<p><p>Extremity tissue expanders are medical devices used in reconstructive surgery to facilitate the growth of soft tissues in the extremities, such as arms and legs. <p><p>The extremity tissue expander market is categorized into four main types: anatomical, round, rectangular, and crescent. Anatomical expanders are designed to mimic the natural shape of body parts, providing a more natural outcome. Round expanders offer uniform expansion, ideal for symmetrical areas. Rectangular expanders are tailored for specific body contours, allowing targeted tissue expansion. Crescent expanders are used in unique cases, offering specialized shapes for precise reconstruction. Each type addresses different surgical needs and patient requirements in reconstructive procedures.</p></p>
