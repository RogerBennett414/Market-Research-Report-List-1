<p><strong>The Adjustable Intraocular Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Foldable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adjustable intraocular lens (IOL) market primarily comprises two types: rigid and foldable lenses. Rigid lenses are made from materials like polymethyl methacrylate, offering durability but requiring larger incisions for implantation. In contrast, foldable lenses are crafted from flexible materials, allowing them to be folded for minimal incision surgery, enhancing patient comfort and recovery time. Both types aim to improve vision post-cataract surgery, with foldable lenses gaining popularity due to their less invasive nature and easier handling during procedures.</p></p>
