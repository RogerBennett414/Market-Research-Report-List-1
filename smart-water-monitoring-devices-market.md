<p><p>Smart water monitoring devices encompass various market types, including Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) water meters, which enable two-way communication for real-time data and management. Communication network meters integrate various communication technologies like cellular, LoRa, or Wi-Fi for enhanced data transmission. Other market types may include specialized sensors and devices that monitor quality and leak detection, contributing to efficient water resource management.</p></p>
