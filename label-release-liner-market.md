<p><p>The label release liner market is primarily divided into two types: paper-based and film-based liners. Paper-based release liners are made from materials like kraft or polyethylene-coated paper, offering a cost-effective option with good printability and versatility. In contrast, film-based release liners, typically made from polyester or polypropylene, provide higher durability, moisture resistance, and clarity, making them ideal for demanding applications. Both types cater to varying needs in labeling, packaging, and adhesive applications, contributing to their unique market segments.</p></p>
