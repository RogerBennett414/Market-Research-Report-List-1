Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of the body, particularly soft tissues. Computed Tomography (CT) scanners combine X-ray images for cross-sectional views. Nuclear imaging systems involve radioactive substances to assess physiological processes. Other devices include specialized imaging technologies.</p></p>
