<p><p>The structural pervious pavement market is categorized into three main types: pervious concrete, porous asphalt, and interlocking concrete pavers. Pervious concrete allows water to flow through its matrix, reducing runoff and enhancing drainage. Porous asphalt, designed with voids for water infiltration, offers similar benefits while maintaining a smooth surface. Interlocking concrete pavers feature gaps between units, enabling water absorption and greenery integration. Collectively, these materials promote sustainable urban development by managing stormwater and improving environmental quality.</p></p>
