<p><p>The electro-mechanical brakes market encompasses various types suited for diverse applications. Single face brakes utilize a single friction face for engagement, offering simplicity. Power off brakes engage automatically when power is removed, ensuring safety. Particle brakes utilize magnetic particles for smooth torque control, while hysteresis power brakes provide resistance through magnetic hysteresis, ideal for precise applications. Multiple disk brakes use multiple friction surfaces to enhance braking performance and heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-power and performance-demanding environments.</p></p>
