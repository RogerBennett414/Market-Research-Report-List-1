<p><p>The pharmaceutical vials market comprises three main types: open vials, sterile vials, and ready-to-use vials. Open vials are designed for non-sterile applications, allowing for the direct dispensing of products. Sterile vials are used for sensitive medications that require strict contamination control and are typically employed in injections. Ready-to-use vials simplify the dispensing process by being pre-sterilized and ready for immediate use, reducing preparation time and minimizing the risk of contamination in clinical settings.</p></p>
