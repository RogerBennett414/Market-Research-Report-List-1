<p><p>The laparoscopic surgery retrieval pouch market is categorized into manual opening and automatic opening types. Manual opening pouches require surgeons to open the pouch manually for specimen retrieval, providing control but potentially increasing handling time. In contrast, automatic opening pouches utilize mechanisms that automatically deploy upon use, enhancing efficiency and reducing the risk of contamination. Both types serve crucial roles in minimally invasive surgeries, facilitating the safe collection of tissue specimens while minimizing patient trauma and recovery time.</p></p>
