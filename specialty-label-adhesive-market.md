<p><p>The specialty label adhesive market primarily consists of two main types: water-based and solvent-based label adhesives. Water-based label adhesives are environmentally friendly, utilizing water as the primary solvent, making them less harmful and easier to clean. They offer excellent adhesion on various surfaces and are ideal for food packaging. In contrast, solvent-based label adhesives use organic solvents, providing stronger bonding and enhanced durability, making them suitable for applications requiring resistance to moisture and potential exposure to chemicals.</p></p>
