<p><p>Drone Identification Systems encompass two main market types: detection and identification technologies, and mitigation technologies. Detection and identification technologies focus on recognizing and tracking the presence of drones in a given area, utilizing radar, radio frequency, and optical systems. Mitigation technologies aim to neutralize potential threats posed by unauthorized drones, employing methods such as jamming, drone capture nets, or directed energy devices. Together, these technologies enhance security by managing the risks associated with drone operations in sensitive environments.</p></p>
