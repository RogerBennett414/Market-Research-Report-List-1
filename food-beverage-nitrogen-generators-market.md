<p><ul><li>PSA Nitrogen Generators</li><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food & Beverage Nitrogen Generators market is primarily divided into two types: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) Nitrogen Generators and Membrane Nitrogen Generators. PSA generators utilize a two-tower system that adsorbs oxygen from air under pressure, producing high-purity nitrogen, ideal for packaging and preservation. In contrast, Membrane generators employ semi-permeable membranes to separate nitrogen from oxygen, providing a more compact and economical solution, but typically with lower purity. Both technologies enhance food safety and extend shelf life.</p></p>
