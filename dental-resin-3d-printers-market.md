<p><p>The dental resin 3D printers market encompasses various technologies, each suited for specific applications. Vat photopolymerization uses a liquid resin cured by light, ideal for high-resolution dental models. Fused deposition modeling extrudes thermoplastic material, offering a cost-effective solution for functional prototypes. PolyJet technology employs inkjet heads to deposit liquid resin, allowing for multi-material and color options. Selective laser sintering fuses powdered material using a laser, suitable for durable dental parts. Other methods continue to emerge, catering to diverse dental needs.</p></p>
