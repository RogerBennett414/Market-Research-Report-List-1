<p><ul><li>Spunlace</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Coform</li><li>Needle Punch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baby wipes market features four main types based on manufacturing methods. Spunlace wipes are produced by bonding fibers with water and pressure, offering softness and strength. Airlaid wipes utilize air to form a fibrous structure, providing high absorbency and a textile-like feel. Coform wipes combine spunlace and airlaid methods, resulting in durability and moisture retention. Needle punch wipes are created by mechanically interlocking fibers, delivering a robust and textured product. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
