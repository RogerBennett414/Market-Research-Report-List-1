<p><p>Mechanical float level sensors are used to detect liquid levels in various applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: switch type and continuous type. Switch type sensors provide discrete level detection, activating a switch when the float reaches a designated level, ideal for simple on/off control. Continuous type sensors, on the other hand, offer ongoing level measurement, providing real-time data on liquid levels. This enables more precise control and monitoring in processes requiring consistent fluid management.</p></p>
