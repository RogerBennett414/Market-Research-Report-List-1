<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Mineral Fluid Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine mineral fluid lubricants market comprises three main types: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants, derived from saturated hydrocarbons, offer excellent thermal stability and low volatility, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Naphthenic lubricants, containing cycloparaffins, provide superior solvency and low-temperature fluidity, enhancing engine efficiency. Aromatic lubricants, rich in unsaturated hydrocarbons, offer high lubrication characteristics but are less common due to environmental concerns. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to varying performance and operational requirements in automotive applications.</p></p>
