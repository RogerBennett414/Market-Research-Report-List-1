<p><p>The laboratory clean bench market encompasses several types designed for maintaining sterile environments. UV clean benches use ultraviolet light to disinfect surfaces and air, effectively reducing microbial contamination. HEPA filter clean benches utilize High Efficiency Particulate Air filters to trap particulate matter, ensuring clean airflow. Vertical laminar flow clean benches direct filtered air downwards, providing a contamination-free working area. Other types may include combination models or specialized benches for unique applications, catering to diverse laboratory needs and protocols.</p></p>
