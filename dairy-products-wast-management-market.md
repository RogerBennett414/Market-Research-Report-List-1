<p><p>Dairy products waste management includes various methods for handling organic waste generated by dairy operations. Aerobic digestion, or composting, involves the breakdown of organic matter in the presence of oxygen, producing compost for soil enhancement. Anaerobic digestion operates without oxygen, generating biogas and digestate, useful for energy and fertilizers. Incineration or combustion involves burning waste to reduce volume and generate energy, while other methods may include thermal treatment or advanced recycling technologies, each providing distinct environmental benefits.</p></p>
