<p><p>The food-grade diaphragm pump market comprises two primary types: Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD) and Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD). AODD pumps use compressed air to create suction and discharge, making them suitable for handling viscous foods without electricity hazards. EODD pumps rely on electric motors, offering precise control and efficiency, ideal for hygienic applications in food processing. Both types ensure safe and reliable fluid handling while meeting stringent regulatory standards for food safety.</p></p>
