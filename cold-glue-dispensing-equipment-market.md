<p><p>Cold glue dispensing equipment is categorized into manual and automatic types. Manual dispensers require operators to apply glue, offering flexibility for small-scale or intricate tasks. They are cost-effective and suitable for varied applications. In contrast, automatic dispensers streamline the process, allowing for consistent and precise glue application in high-volume production. These systems enhance efficiency, reduce waste, and often integrate with production lines, making them ideal for industrial settings. Both types cater to different operational needs and production scales.</p></p>
