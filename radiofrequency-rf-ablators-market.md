<p><p>The Radiofrequency (RF) Ablators market is primarily categorized into two types: temperature-controlled RF ablation devices and fluid-cooled RF ablation systems. Temperature-controlled RF ablation devices utilize thermal feedback mechanisms to maintain optimal temperatures during procedures, enhancing effectiveness and safety. In contrast, fluid-cooled RF ablation systems incorporate a coolant to regulate the temperature of the electrode, allowing for extended lesion creation without overheating. Both types are crucial for minimally invasive treatments in various medical applications, including cardiac and oncological interventions.</p></p>
