<p><p>The Biotechnology Separation Systems market encompasses various techniques for isolating and purifying biological components. Membrane filtration uses selective membranes to separate particles based on size. Liquid chromatography separates compounds based on their interactions with a stationary phase. Centrifuges exploit centrifugal force for separating components by density. Electrophoresis utilizes electric fields to separate molecules based on size and charge. Flow cytometry analyzes individual cells in a fluid stream using lasers. "Others" include innovative methods and technologies not categorized above, enhancing separation efficiency.</p></p>
