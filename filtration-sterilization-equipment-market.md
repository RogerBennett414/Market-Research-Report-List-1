<p><p>Filtration sterilization equipment encompasses various technologies designed to remove contaminants from fluids. Deep filters utilize a thick, porous medium that physically traps particles and microorganisms, making them suitable for larger volumes and high solid loads. In contrast, membrane filters employ a thin barrier with precise pore sizes to selectively allow smaller molecules to pass while retaining pathogens and larger particles. Together, these filtration types cater to diverse applications in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food industries, ensuring product safety and compliance.</p></p>
