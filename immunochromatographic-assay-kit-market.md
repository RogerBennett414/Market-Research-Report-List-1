<p><strong>The Immunochromatographic Assay Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sandwich Assay</li><li>Competitive Assay</li><li>Multiplex Detection Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochromatographic assay kit market includes various types such as sandwich assays, competitive assays, and multiplex detection assays. Sandwich assays utilize two antibodies to capture and detect the target antigen, providing high specificity. Competitive assays involve a competition between the target antigen and a labeled antigen for binding sites on a specific antibody, often resulting in inversely proportional results. Multiplex detection assays allow simultaneous detection of multiple analytes in a single test, enhancing efficiency and data richness in diagnostics.</p></p>
